Tips for a pet-friendly vacation

The key to a successful pet-friendly vacation is preparation.

Keep these tips in mind before hitting the road or taking to the air.

  • Consider the mode of transportation
  • Test your pets travel tolerance
  • Double-check dog-friendly accommodations

Consider the mode of transportation

How you will get to your destination depends on how far away from home you’re going, of course, but you’ll also want to consider your dog’s comfort.


Test your pets travel tolerance

General Tips

  • Acclimate your pet to travel by gradually introducing them to car rides, crate training, and wearing a harness or carrier.
  • Microchip and identify your pet with a collar and tag, and consider adding a temporary travel tag.
  • Pack essential items such as food, water, bowls, utensils, bedding, toys, medications, and a first aid kit.
